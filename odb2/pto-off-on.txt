2026-05-09. PTO toggled on at ~t=5 s.

Part of a side-by-side set documenting attempts to find body-control
signals on this bus (alongside fwd-on-and-off.asc, pto-on-off.asc,
headlights-off-on.asc).

Outcome: **none of the 4WD / PTO / headlight switch states appear on
this bus as a discrete flag.** Every byte was checked across all 62
broadcast IDs and no byte transitions cleanly with any of those
toggles. What headlights and PTO *do* produce is a clean step in F100F3
pack current (the auxiliary load shifts the BMS-reported pack draw);
4WD selection produces no visible signature at all. This is consistent
with those switches being either hard-wired discrete I/O or carried on
a second CAN segment we don't have a tap on. See "Open questions" in
NOTES.txt for the two-bus hypothesis.
